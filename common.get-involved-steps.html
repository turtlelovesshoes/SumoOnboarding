Answer Questions on Social Media

Love Firefox? Love Twitter and Facebook? When our users have questions, the first place a lot of them go is straight to social media. The Social Support team is a community of enthusiastic volunteers helping to solve issues, share our knowledge and build relationships through social media that will empower our users to get the most out of their Firefox experience.
